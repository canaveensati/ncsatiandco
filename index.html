About the Firm

Founded in October 2012 as Sati and Company, the firm has grown into a trusted pillar of financial expertise in Haldwani, Uttarakhand. In March 2026, it transitioned to its present identity NC Sati and Co reflecting an evolved vision and deepened specialisation across Accounting, Taxation, Audit, ROC, GST, and Business Advisory.

Led by CA Naveen Chandra Sati (FCA), the firm serves a diverse clientele ranging from local entrepreneurs to established entities, ensuring every financial goal is met with precision and compliance. Known in spiritual circles as Naveen Krishna Das, CA Sati brings a rare union of professional rigour and spiritual ethics grounded in the conviction that financial practice is not merely about numbers, but about integrity and service.

Our transition from 'Sati and Company' to 'NC Sati and Co' marks our continued commitment to growth, while staying true to the values of transparency, accuracy, and diligence that have defined us since 2012.

About the Founder
